The Voice

MICDS' Upper School newspaper, The Voice, expresses the diverse interests of our community. Through mediums such as writing, drawing, photographing, and even more, The Voice provides individuals with an outlet to connect with the community, while allowing the community to learn more about the individual. No matter the form in which you would like to express yourself, The Voice wants you to share as the MICDS community needs your ideas and insights! Please contact bsneider@micds.org if you ever want to share something with the community, or if you have any questions. The Voice is always eager to hear from you.
