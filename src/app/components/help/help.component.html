What is my Portal URL?

In order to integrate your schedule automatically into MyMICDS.net, we need your Portal URL. This can be found in the the MICDS Portal. Once inserted into the Settings Page, your schedule will automatically populate the Home Page! Read below for instructions on how to retrieve your Portal URL.

What is my Canvas URL?

Your Canvas URL, similar to the Portal URL, allows for integration of your assignments from the MICDS Canvas. Once inserted into the Settings Page, your homework will automatically populate your Digital Planner! Read below for instructions on how to retrieve your Canvas URL.

How do I get my Portal URL?

Just follow these 5 easy steps!

  1. Navigate to the MICDS Portal and log in.

  2. Click on the Calendar tab.

  3. Click on the RSS Feed button in the top corner or the calendar.

  4. To the left of Entire Calendar:, right click on the My Calendars link and click Copy Link Location. (Note that the exact text may differ per browser.)

  5. Navigate to the MyMICDS Settings Page and paste the URL under Portal URL and click save!

    If the Settings page says your 'Entire Calendar' URL is invalid, follow the next step.

  6. Only follow this step if the Settings page says your 'Entire Calendar' URL is invalid. Otherwise, ignore this step.

    If your 'Entire Calendar' URL is invalid, click on Individual Calendar Feeds, right click on the Schedule link and click Copy Link Location. Follow the step above and paste the link in the MyMICDS Settings Page.

    If you're still having problems with the Portal URL, email support@mymicds.net

How do I get my Canvas URL?

Just follow these 5 easy steps!

  1. Navigate to the MICDS Canvas and log in.

  2. Click on the Calendar tab.

  3. Click on the Calendar Feed button in the bottom right corner in the sidebar.

  4. Select the URL in the text box and simply copy it.

  5. Navigate to the MyMICDS Settings Page and paste the URL under Canvas URL and click save!
