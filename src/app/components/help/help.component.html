What are my Portal URLs?

In order to integrate your schedule automatically into MyMICDS.net, we need two of your Portal URLs. These can be found in the the MICDS Veracross Portal. Once inserted into the Settings Page, your schedule data will automatically populate the site! Read below for instructions on how to retrieve your Portal URLs.

What is my Canvas URL?

Your Canvas URL, similar to the Portal URLs, allows for integration of your assignments from the MICDS Canvas. Once inserted into the Settings Page, your homework will automatically populate your digital planner! Read below for instructions on how to retrieve your Canvas URL.

How do I get my Portal URL?

Just follow these 5 easy steps!

  1. Navigate to the MICDS Veracross Portal and log in.

  2. Click on the Preferences tab in the top navigation, then select Calendar Subscriptions.

  3. Click on the My Calendars button to bring up your personal feeds.

  4. To the left of My Calendar, RIGHT CLICK on the Subscribe button and click Copy Link Location. (Note that the exact text may differ per browser.)

  5. Navigate to the MyMICDS Settings Page and paste the URL under Portal 'My Calendar' URL and click save!

    You need to insert one last calendar feed to integrate your schedule.

  6. On Veracross, under the My Calendar feed you just copied and to the left of All Classes, RIGHT CLICK on the Subscribe button and click Copy Link Location. (Note that the exact text may differ per browser.)

  7. Navigate back to the MyMICDS Settings Page and paste the URL under Portal 'My Classes' URL and click save!

    Now you're all done! Next step is integrating your Canvas homework.

How do I get my Canvas URL?

Just follow these 5 easy steps!

  1. Navigate to the MICDS Canvas and log in.

  2. Click on the Calendar tab.

  3. Click on the Calendar Feed button in the bottom right corner in the sidebar.

  4. Select the URL in the text box and simply copy it.

  5. Navigate to the MyMICDS Settings Page and paste the URL under Canvas URL and click save!
